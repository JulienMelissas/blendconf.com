About Cameron

Founder of Authentic Jobs (authenticjobs.com), Co-founder of Spoken (spoken.co), and maker of Structures in Type (typestructures.com). Cameron is a sucker for anything that involves his five sons and sports equipment. He lives in the coastal town of Sarasota, Florida with his lovely wife Suzanne.
